Frequently Asked Questions

What is zkTRumble?

zkTRumble is a decentralized application (DApp) that allows you to participate in on-chain rumble fights with full transparency.

How do I participate in a rumble?

To participate in a rumble, you need to connect your EVM-compatible wallet (such as MetaMask) and register for a rumble GameID through the registration page.

How to add Ternoa L2 zkEVM+ network & Claim test $Caps on my Metamask?

Simply follow this link here, click on add subnet to add ternoa network then claim test $Caps.

What is the cost of participation?

There is no participation cost at the moment but a future Smart Contract evolution may allow this parameter. For now, you just need a few $Caps dust to pay incredibly low gas fees.
